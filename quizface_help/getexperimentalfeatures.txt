getexperimentalfeatures

Returns enabled experimental features.

Result:
  [
     "experimentalfeature"     (string) The enabled experimental feature
     ,...
  ],

Examples:
> zcash-cli getexperimentalfeatures 
> curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "getexperimentalfeatures", "params": [] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8232/
